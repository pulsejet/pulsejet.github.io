About Me

Hi there! I am Varun Patil, a Computer Science Master's student at UCLA starting Fall 2020.

I love trains and aircraft (the reason why I hold a major in Mechanical Engineering), but am principally a developer, with over twelve years of software engineering experience; and I've written code in almost every mainstream programming language out there. Change is my all-time favorite. As the phrase goes, it is truly the only constant in this world. Even the value of π, as we know it, keeps getting more accurate!

I gym, I write and (though people keep doubting that) I'm crazy about food. And I simply love music!
